Meet the Owner

Caring childcare by Chonon

Chonon is an experienced childcare provider who creates a warm, safe, and loving environment where every child feels supported.

Originally from Côte d’Ivoire, she brings a multicultural perspective and speaks French, helping children naturally experience language, kindness, and diversity.

Families appreciate her patience, gentle guidance, and the way she treats children with care — like her own.
