Meet the Owner

Caring, experienced childcare by Chonon.

Chonon creates a warm, safe, and loving environment where each child feels supported, valued, and cared for.

Originally from Côte d’Ivoire, she brings a multicultural perspective and speaks French, helping children naturally experience kindness, diversity, and language.

Families trust her patience, experience, and the way she treats children with genuine care — like her own.
